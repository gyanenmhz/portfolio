About Me

Hello! I'm Gyanendra Maharjan, a passionate and dedicated Visual Effects Artist based in the beautiful country Nepal, with over 11 years of industry experience. I specialize in Compositing, 3D Tracking, and Matte Painting, with a strong focus on bringing imaginative worlds to life and enhancing visuals with seamless detail.

My goal is to bring imaginative worlds and seamless visual enhancements to the screen. I thrive on challenges and enjoy collaborating to achieve stunning results.

With a deep enthusiasm for visual storytelling, I thrive on creative challenges and love collaborating with others to deliver stunning results. I'm highly proficient in industry-standard tools like Nuke, Blender, and After Effects, and I’m currently expanding my skills in Houdini and Maya. Always eager to grow, I stay curious and committed to learning new techniques and technologies in the ever-evolving world of VFX.
